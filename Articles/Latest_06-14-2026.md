**Source:** [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/anthropic-finally-beat-openai-in-business-ai-adoption-but-3-big-threats-could-erase-its-lead)

The June 2026 Ramp AI Index shows Anthropic now leads US enterprise AI adoption at roughly 41% of businesses with paid AI subscriptions, overtaking OpenAI for the first time. ## 4. Turkey Unveils $10 Billion AI Action Plan Through 2030

**Source:** [Türkiye Today](https://www.turkiyetoday.com/business/erdogan-launches-10-billion-ai-plan-to-make-turkiye-a-global-digital-power-by-2030-3221878)

President Erdogan announced a national AI Action Plan (2026-2030) built around four pillars: discovery, use, production, and governance. The plan mobilizes at least $10 billion, mostly from the private sector, to scale Turkey's data center capacity to 1 gigawatt by 2030, train 10,000 advanced AI specialists and 100,000 AI practitioners, and open a National Data Library with over 2,000 public datasets spanning health, agriculture, defense, and e-commerce.

## 5. India's Avataar.ai Launches Varya, a Sovereign Distilled Video AI Model

**Source:** [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/11/cheaper-faster-and-culturally-aware-avataars-video-ai-is-built-for-indias-scale/)

Bengaluru-based Avataar.ai, backed by the IndiaAI Mission, launched Varya — billed as India's first distilled video foundation model. The 14B-parameter model generates video in 4 steps instead of 50, costing roughly $0.005 per second of video (about 27x cheaper than comparable open-source models) while emphasizing cultural context such as local festivals, food, and clothing. Varya will be released open-weight on India's AIKosh portal along with its training data.
