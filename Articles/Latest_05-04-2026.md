## 2. Snap Lays Off ~1,000 Employees, Cites AI Advances

**Source:** [AI News & Artificial Intelligence | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/category/artificial-intelligence/)

Snap CEO Evan Spiegel announced the layoff of approximately 1,000 employees and the closure of over 300 open roles, directly attributing the cuts to "rapid advancements in artificial intelligence." Notably, AI now generates more than 65% of Snap's new code, signaling an accelerating shift in how software companies are staffing engineering functions.

## 4. Brain-Inspired Nanoelectronic Chip Cuts AI Energy Use by Up to 70%

**Source:** [ScienceDaily – Artificial Intelligence](https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/computers_math/artificial_intelligence/)

Researchers engineered a new nanoelectronic device using a modified form of hafnium oxide that mimics the way biological neurons process and store information. The chip could slash the energy consumption of AI inference workloads by up to 70%, a potentially transformative step as data centers strain under growing power demands from large-scale AI deployments.

## 5. Google Rolls Out Sweeping Gemini AI Upgrades Across Workspace

**Source:** [Crescendo AI – Latest AI News & Breakthroughs (2026)](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
