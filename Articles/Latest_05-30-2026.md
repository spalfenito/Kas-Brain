## 2. Pope Leo XIV Publishes *Magnifica Humanitas* — First Papal Encyclical on AI

**Source:** [Vatican News](https://www.vaticannews.va/en/pope/news/2026-05/pope-leo-xiv-first-encyclical-magnifica-humanitas.html) | [Time](https://time.com/article/2026/05/25/pope-leo-encyclical-ai-magnifica-humanitas/)

Pope Leo XIV released his first encyclical, *Magnifica Humanitas* ("Magnificent Humanity"), on May 25, 2026 — a 42,300-word document addressing the ethical, social, and spiritual implications of artificial intelligence. Signed on May 15 — exactly 135 years after *Rerum Novarum* — the text draws deliberate parallels between the Industrial Revolution and the AI age. The 245-paragraph document calls for AI to be "disarmed" of its antihuman potential and warns of dangerous imbalances in digital power, framing AI governance as a matter of human dignity and the common good.
